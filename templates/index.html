Welcome to The Hockey Pool

  • Sign Up

  • To get started with The Hockey Pool, you must first sign up and create an account. This option can be found in the upper right hand corner of the webpage.

    Step 1:

    Click Login/Sign Up

    Step 2:

    Click Sign Up

    Step 3:

    Fill out the signup form and submit

  • Login

  • Step 1:

    After creating an account, the you will be directed to the login page. Fill in the information and click Login

  • Create Team

  • Step 1:

    Find the 'Create Team' link in the tool bar

    Step 2:

    Fill out all information on the form. You must make a selection for all 21 groups

    Step 3:

    Click the 'Submit' button at the bottom of the form to create your team

  • View Standings

  • Step 1:

    After you've successfully created your team, you will be automatically directed to the Team Standings page

    Step 2:

    Alternatively, you can select the 'Team Standings' link in the navigation bar above

  • View Team

  • Step 1:

    To view your team, click on your username (in the upper right-hand corner) and select 'View Team' from the drop-down

    Step 2:

    Once this is clicked (or if the team name is clicked directly in the 'Team Standings' page, you will be brought to your team page
